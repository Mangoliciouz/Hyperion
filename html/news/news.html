  • Hyperion is attending ISE 2020

    Hyperion is attending ISE 2020 at the RAI in Amsterdam. Book a meeting with us for February 11th to 14th.

    Integrated Systems Europe, often referred to as ISE, is the largest AV systems integration show in the world. The annual four-day event, which takes place at the RAI Amsterdam every February, is organised and run by Integrated Systems Events. Integrated Systems Events is a joint venture between AVIXA and CEDIA – the two leading industry associations for the global audiovisual industry.

    Integrated Systems Events produces B2B exhibitions and conferences for the professional audiovisual, electronic systems integration and IT industries. The flagship event is Integrated Systems Europe. In addition there are the Smart Building Conference, Digital Signage Summit Series and Integrated Systems Russia.

  • Hyperion has partnered with Rosetta Telecom

    Hyperion has appointed Rosetta Telecom to provide VOIP telephony services.

    Rosetta Telecom voice over IP (VOIP) telephone systems are individually built with our customers’ needs in mind. Designed from the ground up to provide as many benefits to you without becoming overly complicated and difficult to set up.

    All you need is a reasonably good internet connection and you can run your business communications including phones, voicemail, mobile apps and much more.

    Our system is hosted in multiple data centres for resilience and is backed up and maintained by us so you don’t need to worry about anything.

  • Hyperion's Technical Lead accepted into SCTE

    Hyperion's Technical Lead has been granted membership to the Society of Cable Telecommunications Engineers (SCTE).

    SCTE•ISBE is the premier membership organization for technical cable telecommunications professionals. SCTE•ISBE member benefits, technical training courses, certifications, and information resources focus on providing professionals on every level of the industry with the tools needed to develop and advance their expertise.

  • Hyperion is partnered with OSSY

    Hyperion is partnered with One Stop Super Yachts (OSSY) to provide the latest in communication and fibre technologies to the worlds most exclusive luxury yachts.

    Both in new build and upgrade of super yachts, fibre optic connectivity is increasingly being used as it is less susceptible to the harsh marine environment and inherently more reliable than copper based technologies.
